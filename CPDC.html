Objectives

To motivate and guide students for improvement in Communication skills and overall Personality Development and impart quality training for Aptitude and soft skills development of students and ensure its effectiveness

Functions

  • To identify training agencies and arrange quality training programs for Aptitude and Soft Skills improvement of students.

  • To monitor the effectiveness of training program through continuous monitoring and control and take remedial actions wherever needed.

Personality contest event was organized by Mechanical Engineering department CPDC cell during 18th to 23rd January 2018. Event was organized to provide platform to the young talents to showcase their abilities, express their thoughts, emotions, apply their presence of mind and prepare them for personality/talent based events. Total 29 students were registered for event.

Three months English Curriculum Teaching

Three months English Curriculum Teaching for @180 secondary students (Std.V to Std.VII) of Kanhephata Zillah Parishad Prathamik Shala, Kanhephata was undetaken by CPDC student team of E & Tc Department in association with ISR cell from 13th Aug. to 8th Oct. 2016 on weekly basis. The basic topics covered included introduction to nouns, adjectives, pronouns, verbs, spellings, synonyms, antonyms, pronunciations, numerical count and spellings, Marathi to English translation of sentences and paragraph reading. The student team was directed by Student Coordinator Mruganayana Thorat under the guidance of Mrs. Anjali Shrivastav.
